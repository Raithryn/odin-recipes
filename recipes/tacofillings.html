Simple Taco Fillings

A hearty combination of savory seasoned ground beef and creamy refried beans.

Instructions:

Brown 2 lbs of ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat, adding seasoning and water as directed by seasoning package; drain fat, then stir in refried beans until hot.
