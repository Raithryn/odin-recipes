Stuffed Golden Smores

Delectable smores stuffed with chocolate of choice, perfectly toasted

Instructions:

Start a fire, then toast a marshmallow on a stick over the flame's edge(WATCH THE BOTTOM!!), rotating until entirely golden, then insert a chocolate bar into the center.

    Ingredients

  • Marshmallow
  • Chocolate Bar

    Equipment

  • Flamethrower
  • Stick
  • Hands
