Speedy Stir-Fried Rice with Air-Fried Chicken

Stir-fried rice, crisp vegetables, and tender air-fried chicken for a complete and satisfying meal.

Instructions:

For the rice: pressure cook 1 cup rice with 1/4 cup water and dash of salt on high for 3 minutes; adjust as needed, then mix with sauce. For vegetables: stir-fry in oil over medium-high heat for 6-8 minutes, then simmer with sauce. For chicken: toss raw pieces with sauce, then air fry at 400°F (200°C) for 8-10 minutes per side. Combine all and enjoy!

    Ingredients

  • Long Grain White Rice
  • Water or Broth
  • Salt
  • Vegetables
  • Raw Chicken
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Sauce/s

    Equipment

  • Pressure Cooker(InstantPot)
  • Large Pan or Wok
  • Air Fryer
